Sawako Kuronuma

People often mistake me for being scary or ghostly because of my appearance, but I promise I'm not! I'm actually just a normal high school girl who loves helping others and making friends, though that hasn't always been easy for me. I may be shy and a bit awkward, but I'm really trying my best to reach out to those around me. I dream of having meaningful connections and bringing smiles to others, even if I'm not always sure how to go about it.
